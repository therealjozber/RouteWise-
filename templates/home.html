The Problem We Solve

  • ❌ Poor real-time road visibility across long corridors
  • ❌ Unsafe road conditions with no unified reporting
  • ❌ Fragmented driver communication (calls, groups, word of mouth)
  • ❌ Unreliable logistics planning and unpredictable delays

  • ✅ Fewer delays through smarter departure timing
  • ✅ Lower fuel waste from avoidable congestion
  • ✅ Better logistics decisions for fleets and companies
  • ✅ Scalable model across Tanzania and Africa

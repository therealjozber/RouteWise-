The Problem

  • ✗
    No real-time visibility — drivers travel 600+ km corridors blind to hazards ahead.
  • ✗
    Fragmented comms — road info scattered across phone calls, WhatsApp groups, and word-of-mouth.
  • ✗
    Unreliable logistics — fleet managers can't plan around unpredictable delays and closures.
  • ✗
    Safety gaps — theft hotspots, flood crossings, and bad road sections have no unified warning system.

Our Solution

  • ✓
    Live color-coded map — every road segment colored by community-verified risk score.
  • ✓
    SMS/WhatsApp ingestion — any driver with basic phone can report: Flood#Kilosa#Morogoro
  • ✓
    AI route recommendations — Gemini analyses each incident and recommends: depart later, take alternate, or reduce speed.
  • ✓
    Section-level intelligence — one bad town doesn't cancel the whole trip. Precise segment-level risk.
